Sakuba

Sakuba agriculture offers innovative solutions for your remote agricultural needs completely autonomous farms and farm management.

Clean

All Sakuba deployments feature 100% renewable energy sources, Sakuba is committed to running our business in the most environmentally friendly way possible, and will continue to achieve 100% renewable energy usage.

Automated

Everything we do at Sakuba revolves around automation. Most tasks aren't worth doing manually more than a few times. Here at Sakuba we automated everything from code deployment, to hardware/sensors.
